Experimental Results

We trained a Vision Transformer (ViT) model for deepfake detection using a dataset of 200 images (100 Real, 100 Fake), split into 60% training (120 images) and 40% testing (80 images). The ViT backbone was frozen, and only the classifier head was fine-tuned to optimize performance on our small dataset. The following hyperparameters were used:

  • Input Size: 224 x 224
  • Batch Size: 4
  • Optimizer: Adam
  • Learning Rate: 1e-5
  • Dropout Rate: 0.5
  • Regularization: Weight Decay with 0.02

  • Accuracy: 92%
  • Precision: 90%
  • Recall: 93%
